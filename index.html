    Espresso coffee

    Espresso is an intense and dark Italian coffee drink. An espresso coffee drink consists of a little boiling water with the original ground coffee beans, served in a small cup with its saucer, similar in shape to a Turkish coffee cup, but it is often thick and white in color, and it can be transparent.

    French coffee

    French coffee is coffee with milk, although all countries of the world use it under different names. It is plain coffee with shaken milk and served in a large cup. Each cup of coffee with milk with sugar contains 126 calories, and skim milk can be used to reduce calories and fat.

    Turkish coffee

    Turkish coffee is a type of coffee prepared using very finely ground coffee beans without filtering. Turkish coffee refers to a method of brewing finely ground coffee. Any coffee bean can be used; Arabica coffee varieties are best, but Robusta or a combination of both is also used
